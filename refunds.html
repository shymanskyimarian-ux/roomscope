Refund policy

Fair refunds, no runaround.

Last updated: July 2026

Roomscope Pro is a monthly subscription billed through Paddle, our merchant of record. This page explains exactly when and how you get your money back.

01 ·14-day money-back guarantee

If Roomscope Pro didn't do what you needed, request a refund within 14 days of any charge and we'll refund that charge in full — no questions about why.

02 ·Cancelling future billing

You can cancel the subscription at any time using the link in your Paddle receipt email, or by emailing us. Cancelling stops all future charges; your Pro access stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for.

Charges for periods that have already fully elapsed are not automatically refunded, but if you were billed by mistake or never used the service, contact us — we'll make it right.

03 ·How to request a refund

Email shymanskyi.marian@gmail.com from the address you used to subscribe, or use the "Get help" / support link on your Paddle receipt. Include the order or receipt number from Paddle if you have it.

We aim to respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed by Paddle back to your original payment method; the time it takes to appear depends on your bank or card provider.

04 ·Payments & merchant of record

All payments are processed by Paddle. Paddle is the merchant of record for your purchase, so its buyer terms and refund handling also apply. This policy describes how we, the seller, treat refund requests within that framework.
