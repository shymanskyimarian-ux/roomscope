The workflow

Three steps to a plan you can actually build.

No contractor visit, no spreadsheet. A photo, your measurements, and a plan priced for where you live.

01

Snap and measure

Take a photo of the room and enter its dimensions — length, width, height. Thirty seconds, and every material quantity becomes real math, not a guess from a picture.

02

AI reads the room

Vision picks up the surfaces, their condition, and the work worth doing — painting, patching, flooring, trim, caulking.

03

Get a priced plan

Every material, a price for your region, and step-by-step instructions. Export the whole thing to a clean print-ready PDF.
