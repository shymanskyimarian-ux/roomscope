02 ·Estimates are informational

Quantities are calculated from documented assumptions (waste factors, coverage rates), and catalog prices vary by region and over time. Always verify prices and quantities yourself before purchasing anything.

Roomscope is not professional construction, engineering or contracting advice, and does not replace a qualified professional's assessment of your specific space.

03 ·Safety

The tool deliberately refuses to give DIY guidance for licensed trades — electrical, plumbing, gas and structural work. When it flags such work, hire a licensed professional.

Never attempt licensed-trade work yourself. Electrical, plumbing, gas and structural mistakes can be lethal, and in most places doing this work without a license is illegal.
