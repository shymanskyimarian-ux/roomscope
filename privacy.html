Privacy policy

Your room stays in your browser.

Last updated: July 2026

Roomscope is a static site with no backend. That makes this policy short, because there is very little we could collect even if we wanted to. Here is exactly what happens with your data.

01 ·Photos

Photos you add are processed entirely in your browser. They are never uploaded to any server — ours or anyone else's — because there is no server. When you close the tab, the photo is gone, unless you keep using it locally yourself.

02 ·Local data

Your plan status, project count and any reviews you write live in your browser's localStorage, on your own device. We run no accounts and no user database, so we cannot see, back up or restore this data.

Clearing your browser data (or using a different browser or device) deletes or resets it. That is the whole lifecycle.

03 ·Payments

Payments are handled entirely by Paddle (our merchant of record), on Paddle's own pages. Card details go directly to Paddle; we never see them and never store them. See Paddle's privacy policy (linked on its checkout page) for how it handles your data.

04 ·Analytics

We use privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics (GoatCounter) to see aggregate traffic — roughly how many people visit and which link brought them. It sets no cookies, stores no personal data, and never tracks you across other sites, so no consent banner is needed. We use it only to gauge interest while the product is in early testing.

05 ·Questions
